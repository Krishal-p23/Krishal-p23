## 📌 Projects

### 🛰️ SOHO Magnetogram Enhancement (Deep Learning)
Improved resolution of solar magnetogram images using **Deep Convolutional Neural Networks**, working with scientific imaging data and exploring model reliability in real-world research scenarios.

📄 Paper Link: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11207-026-02619-z

### 🧭 TourSort
A smart travel planning and sorting platform designed to help users efficiently organize and optimize their travel itineraries. The system focuses on improving travel planning through structured recommendations and user-centric design.

🔗 Repository: https://github.com/krishal-p23/toursort

### 🛠️ ServiGO – Service Provider App
A full-stack service marketplace application that connects users with local service providers. The platform streamlines service discovery, booking, and management, aiming to improve accessibility and convenience for everyday services.

🔗 Repository: https://github.com/krishal-p23/serviGO
